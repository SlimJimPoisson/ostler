# Glossary

## Actor

The person, system, or seat required to clear a gate.

Actor answers: who must act?

## Cockpit

The thin coordinating role that classifies, prioritizes, dispatches, reviews, and integrates work.

The cockpit should not become the worker.

## Evidence

The concrete support for a conclusion: files, test results, diffs, commands, outputs, notes, paths, or review artifacts.

## Gate

A specific blocked action.

A gate should be named by what blocks progress, not by who is associated with it.

## Gate absorption

The process of reducing or eliminating an apparent gate by doing all safe precursor work first.

## Mission

A durable workstream that persists across sessions and workers.

## Packet

A bounded work assignment with an owner, expected return artifact, and review path.

## Parked item

A work item intentionally deferred with an owner, reason, and wake condition.

## Snapshot

A readable view of the board suitable for lower-privilege readers or observers.

## Worker

A bounded agent or process that performs a task and returns evidence.
